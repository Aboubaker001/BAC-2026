## Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, free, and accessible English language learning resource for Algerian Baccalaureate students, empowering them to achieve academic excellence and confidently pursue their future endeavors.

## Vision

To become the largest and most trusted open-source platform for English Baccalaureate preparation, fostering a vibrant community of learners and contributors dedicated to educational advancement.

## Who this project is for

This project is designed for Algerian students preparing for the English Baccalaureate exam, as well as educators, tutors, and anyone interested in improving their English language skills within the context of the Algerian curriculum.
